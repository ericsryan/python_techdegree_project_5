About Me

For the past 20 years I have served as a caregiver and care home administrator. I found an interest in web development and computer programming because of a need in my work.

As a caregiver and administrator it is necessary to fill out a considerable amount of paperwork and the information needed to complete the work is usually found in several different places. When a piece of information changes it is often necessary to update the change in several places as well. I thought, "Wouldn't it be great if I could enter a piece of information into a database and that information could automatically be retrieved in order to fill out these forms I fill out repeatedly?" My hope was to find an application that allowed me to do this, but the specific requirements ruled out any solution I was able to find. I felt the need to develop my own application and began to study computer programming.

After much research I felt that the most flexible and accessable way to develop the application would be as a web application. A priority for the application is to make it easy to use for some that may not be as familiar with technology.

My goal is to complete the application that I set out to create, but I also discovered that I really enjoy programming and I am ready to make a career change and pursue programming full time.

I have had the opportunity to take over the hosting and maintainance of about 30 websites. I have built websites for two of these clients, the projects can be found in the portfolio section of this site.
